About Us

Yuri's Dog Grooming is built around calm, patient care for every dog. Whether your pet needs a light bath or a full grooming transformation, we take the time to make them comfortable and look their best.

We believe grooming should be gentle and stress-free. Every appointment includes a gentle bath, careful brushing, nail care, and a style that suits your dog's breed and personality.

Our goal is simple: happy pets, happy owners, and a grooming experience that feels safe and personal.
